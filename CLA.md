## How to sign

You do not sign this file by editing it. When You open Your first pull request, an
automated check ("CLA Assistant") will post a comment. Signing is a one-time action
that covers all Your future Contributions:

- **Individuals:** reply to the bot's pull-request comment with the exact sentence
  it asks for. Your GitHub identity and the timestamp are recorded as Your
  signature.
- **Companies / legal entities:** if You are contributing on behalf of an employer
  or other entity, contact **legal@mailkite.dev** to put a Corporate CLA in place
  before Your Contributions are merged, so that the entity's authorized signatory
  — not just the individual developer — grants the licenses above.
